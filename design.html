'Dark Matter' and 'Allergies' 2021

Journey to...

Initial Sketch

I went for a simple design here that acts as a visual metaphor for the poster's text, 'The stuff that holds the universe together'.

Photo Method

I first took the approach of taking a photo of a real hand and using photoshop to add the universe. This design looked a bit 'corny' to me.

Photoshop Conversion

I converted the real image into a cartoon looking image using photoshop. This design didn't really look how I intended, so I decided to create the whole piece purely on illustrator.

Initial Sketch

I had already finished the 'Dark Matter' poster at this point so I had a pretty clear idea of how I wanted the 'Allergies' one to look. I went with a very minimalistic style sketch.

First Illustration

This design seemed like it was missing something which is why there is more features in the final piece such as the backround's gradient. I also made sure to fix any spelling errors for the final work.

First Variation of Burning Paper

This was the first paper that I burnt. The photo was taken at night which is why the flames are so visible. The flames did appear more dull in my final approach but I think this was worth it.
