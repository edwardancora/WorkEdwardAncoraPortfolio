'Making Salad' 2021

This was actually the first film I made for my current course. We were tasked with making a film under 3 minutes that showcased continuity editing. I learned how to use a SONY NX5 for this task. I didn’t use it very well here as I forgot to turn off the ND filter, this is why the film is fairly dark. This was also my first time using Premiere Pro. The editing process helped me realise how I could have executed the shoot better. I started to understand what makes shots cut well together and what makes them cut poorly together. I think this piece is decent for my first film, but I knew at the time there was a lot to improve on.

'Mental Wellbeing' 2021

For this project, I was tasked with making a documentary style interview sequence. I am overall pretty happy with this work. I think the story is the highlite of this piece. Mental wellbeing is a pretty relevent topic and I think the film gives a decent insight into it. I would change this film a lot if I were to give this project a redo. I would probably decrease the depth of field in the A-Roll to blure the backround and pop Pamela out of the frame more. I would also record Pamela's dialogue in a room with less echo so that I can cut the audio together more easily.

'Hot V Cold' 2021

This was my first animated work. We had to think of two opposite words and create an animated transition between the two of them. I learned how to use After Effects to make this project. The work may not look like much, but I actually spend lots of hard hours making it and I am overall proud of what I've produced. If I could change something, it would probably be the watchtower in the background. It is bright and distracting.
